Spotify does more than you expect from it!!
Do you often find yourself listening to some podcast or song that you never intended to but now you are hinged? Everyone does! Personalized recommendation can work to an extent but to ensure that one user retains, Spotify recommends what is called a mix. Recommending you a mix of what is obtained from your music tastes with popular tastes and then slowly sliding in something that may lie far from your interests. This ensures not to bore the user and keep giving new reasons to stay on the app.
